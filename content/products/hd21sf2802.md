The HD21SF2802 is a high-output 21-inch ferrite subwoofer optimized for sub-bass reinforcement in large venues.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 530 mm (21 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 1800 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 3600 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 97.5 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 34 - 1200 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 152 mm (6.0 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 34 Hz
- **Magnet Type:** Ferrite
