The HD18G401 is a high-sensitivity 18-inch ferrite subwoofer designed for punchy and fast bass reproduction. It is ideal for live sound applications and high-fidelity bass cabinets.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 460 mm (18 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 900 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 1800 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 98.0 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 31 - 1000 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 100 mm (4.0 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 31 Hz
- **Qts (Total Quality Factor):** 0.27
- **Vas (Equivalent Volume):** 340 L
- **Magnet Type:** Ferrite
