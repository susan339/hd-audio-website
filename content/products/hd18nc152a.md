The HD18NC152A is a flagship 18-inch neodymium subwoofer engineered for the most demanding professional audio applications. It features a massive 6-inch (152mm) voice coil and a carbon fiber reinforced cone for unmatched power handling and sonic precision.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 460 mm (18 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 2000 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 4000 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 97.5 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 35 - 1000 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 152 mm (6.0 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 35 Hz
- **Qts (Total Quality Factor):** 0.24
- **Vas (Equivalent Volume):** 145.8 L
- **Magnet Type:** Neodymium
