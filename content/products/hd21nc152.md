The HD21NC152 is a high-performance 21-inch neodymium subwoofer featuring a carbon fiber reinforced cone. Designed for maximum power handling and superior low-frequency extension, it is ideal for large-scale professional touring systems and high-SPL installations.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 530 mm (21 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 2000 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 4000 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 97.0 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 33 - 1200 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 152 mm (6.0 inch)
- **Voice Coil Material:** Copper
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 33 Hz
- **Magnet Type:** Neodymium
- **Cone Material:** Carbon fiber reinforced paper
