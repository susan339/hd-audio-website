The HD21N125A is a high-power 21-inch neodymium subwoofer featuring a 125mm voice coil, designed for efficient bass reinforcement in professional audio systems.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 530 mm (21 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 1200 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 2400 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 98.0 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 35 - 1800 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 125 mm (5.0 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 35 Hz
- **Magnet Type:** Neodymium
