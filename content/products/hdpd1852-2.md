The HD/PD1852-2 is a premier choice for high-SPL applications, offering a very low resonant frequency (30 Hz) and high power handling. It is widely used in premium horn-loaded subwoofer cabinets.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 460 mm (18 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 1000 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 2000 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 98.0 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 30 - 2000 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 125 mm (5.0 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 30 Hz
- **Qts (Total Quality Factor):** 0.19
- **Vas (Equivalent Volume):** 248 L
- **Magnet Type:** Ferrite
