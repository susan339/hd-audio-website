The HD18SF135 is a powerful ferrite-based 18-inch subwoofer utilizing a 5.3-inch (135mm) voice coil. It is built for long-term reliability in high-power PA systems and touring racks.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 460 mm (18 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 1500 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 3000 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 96.5 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 35 - 1000 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 135 mm (5.3 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 35 Hz
- **Qts (Total Quality Factor):** 0.38
- **Vas (Equivalent Volume):** 135 L
- **Magnet Type:** Ferrite
