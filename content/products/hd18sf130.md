The HD18SF130 features a 5.1-inch (130mm) voice coil and a robust ferrite motor. It is designed to provide high-quality low-frequency reinforcement for clubs, theaters, and outdoor events.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 460 mm (18 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 1200 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 2400 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 97.0 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 38 - 1200 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 130 mm (5.1 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 38 Hz
- **Qts (Total Quality Factor):** 0.40
- **Vas (Equivalent Volume):** 155 L
- **Magnet Type:** Ferrite
