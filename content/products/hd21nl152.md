The HD21NL152 is a professional 21-inch neodymium subwoofer designed for high-efficiency low-frequency reproduction. It features a lightweight neodymium motor structure and a high-excursion suspension system.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 530 mm (21 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 1500 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 3000 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 98.0 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 34 - 1500 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 152 mm (6.0 inch)
- **Voice Coil Material:** Copper
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 34 Hz
- **Magnet Type:** Neodymium
