The HD21N150A is a versatile 21-inch neodymium subwoofer offering a balance of high power handling and excellent transient response.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 530 mm (21 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 1500 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 3000 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 97.0 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 35 - 1500 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 150 mm (6.0 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 35 Hz
- **Magnet Type:** Neodymium
