The HD18X401 is a ultra-low frequency 18-inch ferrite subwoofer featuring a 4-inch voice coil. It is designed to deliver high SPL at very low frequencies, making it perfect for cinematic and heavy bass applications.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 460 mm (18 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 1500 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 3000 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 97.5 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 30 - 1000 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 100 mm (4.0 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 30 Hz
- **Qts (Total Quality Factor):** 0.27
- **Vas (Equivalent Volume):** 274 L
- **Magnet Type:** Ferrite
