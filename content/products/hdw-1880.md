The HDW-1880 is a robust 18-inch subwoofer designed for general-purpose professional audio use. It offers a reliable performance and great value for high-power sound reinforcement projects.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 460 mm (18 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 1000 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 2000 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 97.0 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 35 - 1500 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 100 mm (4.0 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 35 Hz
- **Qts (Total Quality Factor):** 0.35
- **Vas (Equivalent Volume):** 210 L
- **Magnet Type:** Ferrite
