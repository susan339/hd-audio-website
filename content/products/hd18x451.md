The HD18X451 is a heavy-duty 18-inch ferrite subwoofer designed for maximum SPL. With a 4.5-inch voice coil and a reinforced paper cone, it is a reliable workhorse for professional sound reinforcement.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 460 mm (18 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 1600 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 3200 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 97.0 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 38 - 1000 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 115 mm (4.5 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 38 Hz
- **Qts (Total Quality Factor):** 0.38
- **Vas (Equivalent Volume):** 113 L
- **Magnet Type:** Ferrite
