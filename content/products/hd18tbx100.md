The HD18TBX100 is a classic 18-inch ferrite subwoofer known for its excellent reliability and smooth frequency response. It is a preferred choice for professional audio installations and portable PA systems.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 460 mm (18 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 1200 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 2400 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 97.0 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 34 - 1000 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 100 mm (4.0 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 34 Hz
- **Qts (Total Quality Factor):** 0.35
- **Vas (Equivalent Volume):** 154 L
- **Magnet Type:** Ferrite
