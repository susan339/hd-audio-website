The HD/PD1845 is inspired by classic professional designs, offering exceptional linearity and low distortion. It is ideal for high-fidelity professional audio systems and studio monitors.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 460 mm (18 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 800 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 1600 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 98.0 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 36 - 2000 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 115 mm (4.5 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 36 Hz
- **Qts (Total Quality Factor):** 0.28
- **Vas (Equivalent Volume):** 178 L
- **Magnet Type:** Ferrite
