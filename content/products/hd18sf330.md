The HD18SF330 is a high-power ferrite subwoofer featuring a massive 330mm magnet system. It is designed for maximum durability and sustained high-SPL output in professional environments.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 460 mm (18 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 2000 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 4000 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 96.0 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 35 - 1000 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 125 mm (5.0 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 35 Hz
- **Qts (Total Quality Factor):** 0.32
- **Vas (Equivalent Volume):** 140 L
- **Magnet Type:** Ferrite (330mm)
