The HD18N455 is a high-efficiency 18-inch neodymium subwoofer designed for high-output touring systems. With a 4.5-inch voice coil and advanced thermal management, it delivers consistent performance under extreme loads.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 460 mm (18 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 1500 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 3000 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 98.0 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 38 - 1200 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 115 mm (4.5 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 38 Hz
- **Qts (Total Quality Factor):** 0.35
- **Vas (Equivalent Volume):** 160 L
- **Magnet Type:** Neodymium
