The HD18NW100 is optimized for very low frequency extension in vented enclosures. Its neodymium motor and specialized cone suspension provide excellent control and deep, punchy bass.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 460 mm (18 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 1200 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 2400 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 98.0 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 35 - 1000 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 100 mm (4.0 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 35 Hz
- **Qts (Total Quality Factor):** 0.22
- **Vas (Equivalent Volume):** 245 L
- **Magnet Type:** Neodymium
