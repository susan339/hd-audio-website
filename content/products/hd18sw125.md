The HD18SW125 is a ultra-high power 18-inch neodymium subwoofer. Designed for the most extreme low-frequency reproduction, it utilizes a 5-inch voice coil and a triple-roll surround for massive excursion.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 460 mm (18 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 2000 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 4000 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 96.5 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 35 - 1000 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 125 mm (5.0 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 35 Hz
- **Qts (Total Quality Factor):** 0.28
- **Vas (Equivalent Volume):** 150 L
- **Magnet Type:** Neodymium
