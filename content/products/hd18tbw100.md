The HD18TBW100 is a high-performance 18-inch ferrite subwoofer featuring a 4-inch (100mm) voice coil and a triple-roll surround. It is designed for deep bass and high power handling in a wide range of applications.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 460 mm (18 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 1500 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 3000 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 96.0 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 34 - 1000 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 100 mm (4.0 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 34 Hz
- **Qts (Total Quality Factor):** 0.35
- **Vas (Equivalent Volume):** 153 L
- **Magnet Type:** Ferrite
