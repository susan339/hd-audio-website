The HD21SF330-2 is a massive 21-inch ferrite subwoofer designed for extreme durability and sustained high-power output. It features a heavy-duty ferrite magnet system and a double-spider suspension.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 530 mm (21 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 2000 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 4000 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 96.0 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 33 - 1200 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 152 mm (6.0 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 33 Hz
- **Magnet Type:** Ferrite
