The HD18N125A features a 5-inch (125mm) voice coil and a powerful neodymium motor assembly. It is optimized for use in compact bass reflex enclosures where high SPL and reliability are required.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 460 mm (18 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 1200 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 2400 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 97.0 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 40 - 1500 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 125 mm (5.0 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 40 Hz
- **Qts (Total Quality Factor):** 0.32
- **Vas (Equivalent Volume):** 180 L
- **Magnet Type:** Neodymium
