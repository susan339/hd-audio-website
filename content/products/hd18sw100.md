The HD18SW100 is a lightweight, high-performance neodymium subwoofer featuring a 4-inch (100mm) voice coil. It is designed for applications where weight is a critical factor without compromising on SPL.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 460 mm (18 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 1200 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 2400 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 97.5 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 40 - 1500 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 100 mm (4.0 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 40 Hz
- **Qts (Total Quality Factor):** 0.38
- **Vas (Equivalent Volume):** 190 L
- **Magnet Type:** Neodymium
