The HD18X455 is an ultra-long excursion 18-inch neodymium subwoofer. It features an optimized suspension system to maintain linearity at extreme power levels, delivering clean and accurate bass.

### Detailed Specifications:
- **Nominal Diameter:** 460 mm (18 inch)
- **Rated Impedance:** 8 ohm
- **Nominal Power Handling (AES):** 1600 W
- **Maximum Power Handling:** 3200 W
- **Sensitivity (1W/1m):** 96.0 dB
- **Frequency Response:** 35 - 1000 Hz
- **Voice Coil Diameter:** 115 mm (4.5 inch)
- **Fs (Resonant Frequency):** 35 Hz
- **Qts (Total Quality Factor):** 0.35
- **Vas (Equivalent Volume):** 145 L
- **Magnet Type:** Neodymium
